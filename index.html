About us

PT. Data Raya Solusi (Gasys) has been the leading provider of Dealer Management System Company in Indonesia. Data Raya Solusi has been established since 2006. We provide numerous products to meet all your technology needs. We are automotive practitioners, blend creative, strategic planning with expert knowledge to tackle the toughest challenges for dealer businesses. We focus on Dealer Management System. Our Teams are fully certified and highly experienced to face the diverse challenges in automotive business process.
